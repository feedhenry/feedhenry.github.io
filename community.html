FeedHenry is open source and open for contributions. There are many ways to get involved, and everyone is invited to discuss, collaborate, and connect with us.

Discuss

IRC

Our day-to-day online communication happens via IRC. Join the discussion:

#feedhenry on the freenode network.

Mailing Lists

For longer form discussion or topics that don't need an immediate response, we use the feedhenry-dev mailing list.

For ease of use, we've configured a nabble mirror that we recommend using.

Collaborate

GitHub

Development for FeedHenry happens across a number of repositories. They're grouped under several GitHub organizations:

feedhenry
feedhenry-templates
feedhenry-raincatcher

JIRA

We use JIRA for issue tracking, organizing development sprints, and planning our road map. View the list of open issues.

Connect

Blog

Visit our blog to get the latest updates on what is happening in the FeedHenry project
